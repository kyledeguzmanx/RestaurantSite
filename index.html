History

Csun Burger Grill© is a family owned quick serve hamburger chain,which was founded by 2010. Our first store was opened in Hollywood and after that each year we opened a store around California. Currently Csun Burger Grill has more than 11 restaurants around California and in each one of our store we mainly focus on serving fresh hamburgers made with highest quaility of ingredients.
Since 2010 Csun Burger Grill has become known for deliciously fresh cooked hamburgers and Milkshakes.During Covid-19 are restaurants became known as one of the best food stores where the health guidlience are followed perfectly.
